Overview

Rated as the 17th largest company in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in 2001, Jadawel's primary objectives have been the design, construction, operation and management of purpose-built residential communities with recreation services and facilities, hard and soft furnishings and security for lease to international clientele. The construction of our facilities represents our business philosophy, a commitment to premium quality construction and design, with American-style interiors and Arabic-style exteriors. All furniture and appliances which are of US origin provide our tenants with a familar environment. Hand -in-hand with our quality construction, we operate and maintain our facilities to 5-star hotel standards including food and beverage, housekeeping, management services, telephone/water/electricity, transportation, and leisure facilities. Each "community" provides swimming pools, tennis, squash and racquetball courts, bowling alleys, gymnasiums, jacuzzis, saunas, table tennis and billiards rooms, kid's entertainment centre, supermarket, dining facilities, multi purpose theatres, library, supermarket, hairdressers and post office. In short, we provide a full-service package without equal in Saudi Arabia.
